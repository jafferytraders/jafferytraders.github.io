Company Profile

Jaffery Traders, based in the Sindh Industrial Trading Estate (SITE) area of Karachi, Pakistan, is a leading supplier of electro-plating and surface finishing services to various industries. Our experience in this field exceeds well over 15 years.

Jaffery Traders was founded in 2003 and was originally a sister company of Azhad Industries Co. LLC based in Dubai, UAE. Since formation, Jaffery Traders has become the leading supplier of electro-plating services to the automotive sector in Pakistan. We provide surface finishing services for different metals ranging from Mild Steel (MS), Stainless Steel (SS), Aluminum and Brass.

The company's priority throughout the years has been innovation and continuous improvement. This philosophy has enabled us to deliver impeccable quality products and services to always exceed the expectations of our customers.
